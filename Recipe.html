Delicious Creamy Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  3. Add cream and cheese, stirring well.
  4. Mix the pasta into the sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.
